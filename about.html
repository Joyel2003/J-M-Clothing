About Us

Founded in 2024, J&M is a Indian multinational clothing retail company known for its fast-fashion clothing for men, women, teenagers, and children. J&M operates in 24 countries with over 5,000 stores and is one of the largest global clothing retailers.

Our mission is to offer fashion and quality at the best price in a sustainable way. We believe in making sustainable, good-quality fashion accessible to as many people as possible.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At J&M, we're committed to leading the change towards a more sustainable fashion industry. We work towards sustainable production, fair working conditions, and reduced environmental impact throughout our entire value chain.

Some of our sustainability initiatives include using organic and recycled materials, reducing water usage, and ensuring fair wages and safe working conditions for factory workers.
